Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

TimeLab is designed to protect your privacy. We collect minimal information:

  • Learning Progress: Stored locally in your browser only
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous usage data via Google Analytics
  • Performance Data: Anonymous session recordings via Microsoft Clarity

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Improve the educational experience
  • Understand how children learn to tell time
  • Fix bugs and enhance features
  • Display relevant educational advertisements

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share personal information. Anonymous analytics data is shared with:

  • Google Analytics: For usage statistics
  • Microsoft Clarity: For user experience insights
  • Google AdSense: For displaying educational advertisements

4. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

TimeLab is designed for children and complies with COPPA:

  • No personal information is collected from children
  • All progress is stored locally on the device
  • No registration or accounts required
  • Parents can clear all data by clearing browser storage

5. Cookies and Local Storage

We use:

  • Local Storage: To save learning progress locally
  • Analytics Cookies: To understand usage patterns
  • Advertising Cookies: To display relevant ads

6. Third-Party Services

TimeLab uses these third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: Privacy Policy
  • Google AdSense: Privacy Policy
  • Microsoft Clarity: Privacy Policy

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear all stored learning progress
  • Opt out of analytics tracking
  • Block advertising cookies
  • Request information about data collection

8. Data Security

We protect your information by:

  • Using HTTPS encryption for all communications
  • Storing data locally when possible
  • Following security best practices
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or TimeLab's data practices, please contact us through our website.

TimeLab is committed to providing a safe, educational, and privacy-friendly learning environment for children.
