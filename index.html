Frequently Asked Questions

What age group is TimeLab designed for?

TimeLab is perfect for children ages 5-12 who are learning to read analog clocks. However, it's also useful for anyone wanting to practice time-telling skills, including adults learning English or reviewing basic concepts.

Is TimeLab really free to use?

Yes! TimeLab is completely free with all features available. We believe quality educational tools should be accessible to everyone. The app is supported by minimal, non-intrusive advertising.

What learning modes are available?

TimeLab offers 5 interactive modes: Normal Clock, Interactive Mode (draggable hands), Learn Mode (progressive lessons), Quiz Mode (timed challenges), and Games Mode (fun educational games). Each mode is designed to reinforce different aspects of time learning.

Can teachers use this in the classroom?

Absolutely! TimeLab is perfect for classroom use. Teachers can project it on smartboards, use it for individual student practice, or assign it for homework. The progress tracking helps monitor student advancement through different time concepts.

Does it work on mobile devices?

Yes! TimeLab is fully optimized for mobile devices, tablets, and computers. The responsive design ensures a great experience on any screen size, making it perfect for learning on-the-go.

How do I track my child's progress?

TimeLab automatically saves progress locally on your device. You can see completion stars for each lesson, quiz scores, and overall advancement through the learning modules. Progress is maintained across sessions for continuous learning.
