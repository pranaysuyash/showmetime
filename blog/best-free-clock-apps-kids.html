Tips for Parents and Teachers

Regardless of which app you choose, here are expert tips for success:

  1. Start with physical clocks: Apps should supplement, not replace, hands-on learning
  2. Practice daily: 10-15 minutes of consistent practice beats hour-long sessions
  3. Connect to real life: Relate digital practice to actual clocks in your environment
  4. Celebrate progress: Acknowledge small wins to maintain motivation
  5. Be patient: Every child learns at their own pace
